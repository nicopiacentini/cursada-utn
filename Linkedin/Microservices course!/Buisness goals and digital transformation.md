# Digital transformation: What is the goal?
- strategy and innovation.
Basically, discover breakthroughs that change de way your software is. It's about doing it differently. **Differentiation as innovation is a strategic business goal that is achieved by continous and tenacious improvement**.
### SW architecture
- It is a structure within a structure. It is a system composed of subsystems. 
- We segregate/separate components that operate independently.
#####  Components
- Some comunicate locally and others globaly. 
- Loosely coupled (poco acoplados) componetns are best -> cuz they can be replaced
##### Quality attributes may change
- Security, privacy, scale and resilience may change over time
- Performance and latency may also change
##### Functional capabiliteis change as well
- Businesses change
- Demands change
- And people change
##### Changes ocurr based on new information
The more we find out and innovate on a sphere, the more changes there will be on the software. It does not matter where the innovation happens, it matters how it will change, quality attributes, components and sw architecture. **The architecture has to support all changes**
